Tanjore Paintings

It is the native art form of 'Thanjavur' also known as 'Tanjore' city of Tamil Nadu. It is one among the famous classical forms of South Indian paintings. These paintings are distinguished from other types of paintings due to the usage of vibrant colors, dense compositions and surface richness.
